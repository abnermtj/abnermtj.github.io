Situation

Queue times in supermarkets really are annoying. My team thought, "Why don't we make an app to solve that?". So we did and created a mobile self-checkout application. This was the first time I played UX designer and I learnt a lot about iterating designs with user feedback. The final result is the high fidelity prototype to the right/above.
