Project Summary

Languages: Python 3, C++
Tech: Sklearn, WeMos, MQTT
Timeline: Aug 2022 - Nov 2022
Role: ML Engineer
Team Size: 5

Situation

The elderly are high risk individuals who face issues such as high fall risk and weaker immune systems due to their age. Because of this, some require dedicated care takers. However in Singapore, old folks' homes are generally understaffed and elderlies may not get the attention they need.

What I did

I created ML models for Fall detection and Severity. Using a MPU9250, I obtained 6-DOF (accelerometer and gyroscope) measurements of various falls. Then I did feature selection from this data and trained a RandomForestClassifier and RandomForestRegressor that could run directly on a WeMOS R1 D2. In the end, I achieved an accuracy 99.0% with a model running directly on a micro-controller.
