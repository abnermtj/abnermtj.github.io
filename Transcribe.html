Summary

A software made I make my life easier. I had many videos i wanted subtitles for but was gated by paid software online.

  • Hugging Face (Slow Free tier)
  • GitHub

What I did

In the transcribe page we have options to customize our transcribe. After those are set, we can batch upload numerous files. They can be a mixture of video and audio, it will automatically be converted to the correct format before we transcribe. One by One, each file will get subtitles with a progress bar at the bottom. At the end, we can hit the download button for all our files.

In the history page, we can see all our previous transcriptions in a nice table as well as download any of them.
